Color Story

For this assignment I need to conduct research and use my findings to generate a project of my choices. Each student was assigned a color to make two components, one analog and one digital. No restriction of formats.

My color is grey(gray).



1.Research ( I made a slide show to represent my idea) 2.digital

Since my analog part maybe is a little bit confused to start, I made an introduction website to help people understanding why I made the game. Please click this link to see the website 3.Analog

I finally decided to use moral dillemma as an inspiration to represent that the grey part of the world, because we usually believe white and black as an metaphor of right and wrong, good and bad. When people choose the option and interact with my installation, they will find out that there is no answer of each moral dillemma.

Each moral dillemma has different meaning. Therefore, I try to combine the story with different paper mechanism.
